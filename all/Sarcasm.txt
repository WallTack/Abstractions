								<div class="dName">Webster's Revised Unabridged Dictionary</div>
				<div class="etymology">
					F. <span class="wUets">sarcasme</span>, L. <span class="wUets">sarcasmus</span>, Gr. <span class="wUgrk">sarkasmo`s</span>, from <span class="wUgrk">sarka`zein</span> to tear flesh like dogs, to bite the lips in rage, to speak bitterly, to sneer, fr. <span class="wUgrk">sa`rx</span> <span class="wUgrk">sa`rkos</span>, flesh
				</div>

				<div class="etymology">

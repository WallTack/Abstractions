				<div class="etymology">
					Through French, fr. L. <span class="wUets">conceptus</span>, a conceiving, conception, fr. <span class="wUets">concipere</span>, to conceive: cf. OF. p. p. nom. <span class="wUets">conciez</span>, conceived. See <span class="wUer">Conceive</span>, and cf. <span class="wUer">Concept</span> <span class="wUer">Deceit</span>

				<div class="etymology">
					Through a Fr. form <i>conceit</i>, from L. <i>conceptus</i>, pa.p. of <i>concipĕre</i>.

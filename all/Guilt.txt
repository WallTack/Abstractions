			<div id="etymology" class="subContainer">
								<div class="dName">Webster's Revised Unabridged Dictionary</div>
				<div class="etymology">
					OE. <span class="wUets">gilt</span>, <span class="wUets">gult</span>, AS. <span class="wUets">gylt</span>, crime; probably originally signifying, the fine or mulct paid for an offence, and afterward the offense itself, and akin to AS. <span class="wUets">gieldan</span>, to pay, E. <span class="wUets">yield</span>,. See <span class="wUer">Yield</span> (v. t.)

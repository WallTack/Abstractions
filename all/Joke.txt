			<div id="idioms" class="subContainer">
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Broke as a joke and it ain't funny</span> - This idiom in my opinion describes how it's not funny to be without a cent and just uses broke and joke as rhyming words that help explain this idiom a lot better.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Butt of a joke</span> - If something or someone becomes the butt of a joke it or they are not taken seriously anymore.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Practical joke</span> - A practical joke is a trick played on someone that is meant to be funny for people watching, though normally embarrassing for the person being tricked.

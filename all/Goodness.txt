					<span>As good as new</span> - If something has been used but is still in extremely good condition, it is as good as new.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Deliver the goods</span> - Do what is required, come up to expectations. For example, Kate delivered the goods and got us the five votes we needed. This phrase alludes to delivering an order of groceries or other items. [Colloquial; second half of 1800s]
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Give as good as you get</span> - If you give as good as you get, you are prepared to treat people as badly as they treat you and to fight for what you believe.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Good antennae</span> - Someone with good antennae is good at detecting things.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Good as gold</span> - If children are as good as gold, they behave very well.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Good egg</span> - A person who can be relied on is a good egg. Bad egg is the opposite.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Good fences make good neighbours</span> - This means that it is better for people to mind their own business and to respect the privacy of others. ('Good fences make good neighbors' is the American English spelling.)
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Good hand</span> - If you are a good hand at something, you do it well.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Good offices</span> - Good offices is help and support, especially in mediating in a dispute.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Good Samaritan</span> - A good Samaritan is a persoon wh helps others in need.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Good shape</span> - If something's in good shape, it's in good condition. If a person's in good shape, they are fit and healthy.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Good spell</span> - A spell can mean a fairly or relatively short period of time; you'll hear weather forecasts predict a dry spell. Sports commentators will say that a sportsperson is going through a good spell when they're performing consistently better than they normally do.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Good time</span> - If you make good time on a journey, you manage to travel faster than you expected.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Good to go</span> - Someone or something that meets one's approval. 'He is good to go.' 'The idea you had is good to go.'
				</div>

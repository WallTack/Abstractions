								<div class="dName">Webster's Revised Unabridged Dictionary</div>
				<div class="etymology">
					F. <span class="wUets">comédie</span>, L. <span class="wUets">comoedia</span>, fr. Gr. ;  a jovial festivity with music and dancing, a festal procession, an ode sung at this procession (perh. akin to  village, E. <span class="wUets">home</span>,) +  to sing; for comedy was originally of a lyric character. See <span class="wUer">Home</span>, and <span class="wUer">Ode</span> 
				</div>

				<div class="etymology">

									<h2 id="_idioms" class="header">Idioms</h2>
			<div id="idioms" class="subContainer">
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Economical with the truth</span> - (UK) If someone, especially a politician, is economical with the truth, they leave out information in order to create a false picture of a situation, without actually lying.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Honest truth</span> - If someone claims that something is the honest truth, they wish to sound extra-sincere about something.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Truth will out</span> - Truth will out means that, given time, the facts of a case will emerge no matter how people might try to conceal them.
				</div>

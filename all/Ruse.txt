			<div id="etymology" class="subContainer">
								<div class="dName">Webster's Revised Unabridged Dictionary</div>
				<div class="etymology">
					F., fr. OF. <span class="wUets">reüser</span>, <span class="wUets">rehuser</span>, to turn aside, to shuffle, retreat, fr. L. <span class="wUets">recusare</span>, to refuse; pref. <span class="wUets">re-</span>, again + <span class="wUets">causa</span>, cause. See <span class="wUer">Cause</span>, and cf. <span class="wUer">Recusant</span>

					O. Fr. <i>ruse</i>—<i>ruser</i>, <i>reūser</i>, to get out of the way—L. <i>recusāre</i>, to decline.

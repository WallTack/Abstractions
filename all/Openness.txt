									<h2 id="_idioms" class="header">Idioms</h2>
			<div id="idioms" class="subContainer">
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Eye-opener</span> - Something surprising, unexpected which reveals the truth about something or someone.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Keep your options open</span> - If someone's keeping their options open, they aren't going to restrict themselves or rule out any possible course of action.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Open all hours</span> - If a shop or suchlike is open all hours, it only closes, if at all, terribly late.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Open book</span> - If a person is an open book, it is easy to know what they think or how they feel about things.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Open old sores</span> - When a sore is almost healed, and if a person rips or tears it open, it is way of preventing the healing process and further aggravating the pain. This phrase, metaphorically suggests, to revive or reopen a quarrel or enmity which was almost forgotten.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Open old wounds</span> - If you open old wounds, you revive a quarrel or problem that caused a lot of trouble in the past.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Open secret</span> - An open secret is something that is supposed to be secret but is common knowledge.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Open-and-shut</span> - A question or issue that is open-and-shut is easily proved or settled.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Opening a can of worms</span> - If you open a can of worms, you do something that will cause a lot of problems and is, on balance, probably going to cause more trouble than it's worth.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Pushing at an open door</span> - If you're pushing at an open door, you achieve what you want easily because many people agree with you or support you.
				</div>

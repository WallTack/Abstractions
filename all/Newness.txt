								<div class="idioms">
					<span>As good as new</span> - If something has been used but is still in extremely good condition, it is as good as new.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>As neat as a new pin</span> - This idiom means tidy and clean.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>New blood</span> - If something needs new blood, it has become stale and needs new ideas or people to invigorate it.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>New brush sweeps clean</span> - 'A new brush sweeps clean' means that someone with a new perspective can make great changes. However, the full version is 'a new brush sweeps clean, but an old brush knows the corners', which warns that experience is also a valuable thing. Sometimes 'broom' is used instead of 'brush'.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>New kid on the block</span> - A new kid on the block is a person who has recently joined a company, organisation, team, etc, and does not know how things work yet.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>New lease of life</span> - If someone finds new enthusiasm and energy for something, they have a new lease of life.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>New man</span> - (UK) A New man is a man who believes in complete equality of the sexes and shares domestic work equally.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>New sheriff in town</span> - This is used when a new authority figure takes charge.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>New York minute</span> - (USA) If something happens in a New York minute, it happens very fast.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Turn a new leaf</span> - If someone turns a new leaf, they change their behaviour and stop doing wrong or bad things.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Whole new ball game</span> - If something's a whole new ball game, it is completely new or different.
				</div>

									<h2 id="_idioms" class="header">Idioms</h2>
			<div id="idioms" class="subContainer">
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Cling to hope</span> - If people cling to hope, they continue to hope though the chances of success are very small.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Cross my heart and hope to die</span> - People say this to show how sincere their promise is.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Glimmer of hope</span> - A glimmer of hope is the belief that there is a slight chance that something positive will happen.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Great white hope</span> - Someone who is expected to be a great success is a great white hope.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Hope against hope</span> - If you hope against hope, you hope for something even though there is little or no chance of your wish being fulfilled.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Hope in hell</span> - If something hasn't got a hope in hell, it stands absolutely no chance of succeeding.
				</div>

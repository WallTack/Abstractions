				<div class="etymology">
					OE. <span class="wUets">merci</span>, F. <span class="wUets">merci</span>, L. <span class="wUets">merces</span>, <span class="wUets">mercedis</span>, hire, pay, reward, LL., equiv. to <span class="wUets">misericordia</span>, pity, mercy. L. <span class="wUets">merces</span>, is probably akin to <span class="wUets">merere</span>, to deserve, acquire. See <span class="wUer">Merit</span>, and cf. <span class="wUer">Amerce</span> 
				</div>

				<div class="etymology">
					Fr. <i>merci</i>, grace—L. <i>merces</i>, <i>mercedis</i>, pay, in later L. also 'favour.'

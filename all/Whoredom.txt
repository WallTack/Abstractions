												<h2 id="_etymology" class="header">Etymology</h2>
			<div id="etymology" class="subContainer">
								<div class="dName">Webster's Revised Unabridged Dictionary</div>
				<div class="etymology">
					OE. <span class="wUets">hordom</span>,; cf. Icel. <span class="wUets">hrdmr</span>, 
				</div>	
								<div class="dName">Chambers's Twentieth Century Dictionary</div>
				<div class="etymology">
					Ice. <i>hóra</i>, an adulteress, fem. of <i>hórr</i>, an adulterer. The word was confused with A.S. <i>horu</i> (Old High Ger. <i>horo</i>), dirt. There is no connection with <i>hire</i>.

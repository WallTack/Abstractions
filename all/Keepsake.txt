As keepsakes of unperished love,<br>

						</div>

							Did some lodge here<br>
And leave for a keepsake<br>
Wisteria trousers? Thoroughworts,<br>
Impossible to forget,<br>
Their fragrance lingers on.
							<div class="poet">"Did some lodge here" by <span>Ki no Tsurayuki</span></div>
						</div>
											<div class="poemQuote">

His little present is sure to send;<br>
Every year, wheresoe'er we be,<br>
He wants a keepsake from you and me.
							<div class="poet">"Our Oldest Friend" by <span>Oliver Wendell Holmes</span></div>
						</div>
											<div class="poemQuote">
							The heart hath its own memory, like the mind,<br>
And in it are enshrined<br>

Now under the pine-trees he is dust.<br>
His golden keepsake bought us wine.<br>
Remembering, the tears run down my cheeks.

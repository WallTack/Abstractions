L. <span class="wUets">blasphemare</span>, to blaspheme, LL. also to blame, fr. Gr. <span class="wUgrk">blasfhmei^n</span> to speak ill, to slander, to blaspheme, fr. <span class="wUgrk">bla`sfhmos</span> evil speaking, perh, for <span class="wUgrk">blapsi`fhmos</span>; <span class="wUgrk">bla`psis</span> injury (fr. <span class="wUgrk">bla`ptein</span> to injure) + <span class="wUgrk">fh`mh</span> a saying, fr. <span class="wUgrk">fa`nai</span> to say.

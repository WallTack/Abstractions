									<h2 id="_idioms" class="header">Idioms</h2>
			<div id="idioms" class="subContainer">
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Haste makes waste</span> - This idiom means that if you try to do something quickly, without planning it, you're likely to end up spending more time, money, etc, doing it.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Lay waste</span> - To lay waste to something is to destroy it.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Waste not, want not</span> - If you don't waste things, you are less likely to end up lacking.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Waste of skin</span> - If a person is referred to as a 'waste of skin', it means he is not worth very much.
				</div>

									<h2 id="_idioms" class="header">Idioms</h2>
			<div id="idioms" class="subContainer">
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>As mad as a hatter</span> - This simile means that someone is crazy or behaves very strangely. In the past many people who made hats went insane because they had a lot of contact with mercury.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>As mad as a wrongly shot hog</span> - (USA) If someone is as mad as a wrongly shot hog, they are very angry. (Same as, Angry as a bear or Angry as a bull).
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Hopping mad</span> - If you're hopping mad, you are extremely angry.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Mad as a badger</span> - If someone is as mad as a badger, they are crazy.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Mad as a bag of hammers</span> - Someone who is as mad as a bag of hammers is crazy or stupid. ('Daft as a bag of hammers' is also used.)
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Mad as a cut snake</span> - (AU) One who is mad as a cut snake has lost all sense of reason, is crazy, out of control.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Mad as a hornet</span> - (USA) If someone is as mad as a hornet, they are very angry indeed.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Mad as a March hare</span> - Someone who is excitable and unpredictable is as mad as a March hare.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Mad as a wet hen</span> - If someone is as mad as a wet hen, they are extremely angry.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Method in his madness</span> - If there's method in someone's madness, they do things in a strange and unorthodox way, but manage to get results.

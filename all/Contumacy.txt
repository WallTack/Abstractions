			<div id="etymology" class="subContainer">
								<div class="dName">Webster's Revised Unabridged Dictionary</div>
				<div class="etymology">
					L. <span class="wUets">contumacia</span>, fr. <span class="wUets">contumax</span>, <span class="wUets">-acis</span>, insolent; prob. akin to <span class="wUets">contemnere</span>, to despise: cf. F. <span class="wUets">contumace</span>,. Cf. <span class="wUer">Contemn</span>

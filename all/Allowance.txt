								<div class="dName">Chambers's Twentieth Century Dictionary</div>
				<div class="etymology">
					O. Fr. <i>alouer</i>, to grant—L. <i>ad</i>, to, and <i>locāre</i>, to place.—<b>Allow</b>, in the sense of <i>approve</i> or <i>sanction</i>, as used in <i>B.</i> and by old writers, has its root in L. <i>allaudāre</i>—<i>ad-</i>, and <i>laudāre</i>, to praise.

					OE. <span class="wUets">hurten</span>, <span class="wUets">hirten</span>, <span class="wUets">horten</span>, <span class="wUets">herten</span>,; prob. fr. OF. <span class="wUets">hurter</span>, <span class="wUets">heurter</span>, to knock, thrust, strike, F. <span class="wUets">heurter</span>,; cf. W. <span class="wUets">hyrddu</span>, to push, drive, assault, <span class="wUets">hwrdd</span>, a stroke, blow, push; also, a ram, the orig. sense of the verb thus perhaps being, to butt as a ram; cf. D. <span class="wUets">horten</span>, to push, strike, MHG. <span class="wUets">hurten</span>, both prob. fr. Old French

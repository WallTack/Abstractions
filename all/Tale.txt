									<h2 id="_idioms" class="header">Idioms</h2>
			<div id="idioms" class="subContainer">
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Old wive's tale</span> - A proverb or piece of advice that is commonly accepted as truth and is handed down the generations, but is normally false.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Tale of the tape</span> - This idiom is used when comparing things, especially in sports; it comes from boxing where the fighters would be measured with a tape measure before a fight.
				</div>

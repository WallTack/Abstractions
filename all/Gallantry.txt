Our heart's devotion cannot try;<br>

						</div>

							IF I had a lover, now, who would he be?<br>
Yourself with your laughter, your gay gallantry?<br>
Yet I'd know when you kissed me your heart was not mine<br>
But kneeling in tears at a lost lady's shrine.
							<div class="poet">"Words To An Irish Air" by <span>Aline Murray Kilmer</span></div>
						</div>

						</div>

Hear how two lovers boastingly contend ;<br>
Like thee successful, such their bloomy youth,<br>
Renown'd alike for gallantry and truth.

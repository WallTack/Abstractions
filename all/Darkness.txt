									<h2 id="_idioms" class="header">Idioms</h2>
			<div id="idioms" class="subContainer">
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>All cats are grey in the dark</span> - Things are indistinguishable in the dark so appearances don't matter.('All cats are grey at night' is also used.)
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Dark horse</span> - If someone is a dark horse, they are a bit of a mystery and we don't know how they will react or perform.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>In the dark</span> - If you're in the dark, you don't know what is happening around you.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Left in the dark</span> - If you are left in the dark about something, you aren't given the information that you should have.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Shot in the dark</span> - If you have a shot in the dark at something, you try something where you have little hope of success.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Whistling in the dark</span> - If someone is whistling in the dark, they believe in a positive result, even though everybody else is sure it will not happen.
				</div>

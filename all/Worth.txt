									<h2 id="_idioms" class="header">Idioms</h2>
			<div id="idioms" class="subContainer">
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>A picture is worth a thousand words</span> - A picture can often get a message across much better than the best verbal description.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>An ounce of prevention is worth a pound of cure</span> - This expression means that is is better to try to avoid problems in the first place, rather than trying to fix them once they arise.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Bird in the hand is worth two in the bush</span> - 'A bird in the hand is worth two in the bush' is a proverb meaning that it is better to have something that is certain than take a risk to get more, where you might lose everything.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Blood is worth bottling</span> - (AU) If an Australian says to you "Your blood is worth bottling", he/she is complimenting or praising you for doing something or being someone very special.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Cake's not worth the candle</span> - If someone says that the cake's not worth the candle, they mean that the result will not be worth the effort put in to achieve it.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Not worth a red cent</span> - (USA) If something is not worth a red cent, it has no value.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Not worth a tinker's dam</span> - This means that something is worthless and dates back to when someone would travel around the countryside repairing things such as a kitchen pot with a hole in it. He was called a 'tinker'. His dam was used to stop the flow of soldering material being used to close the hole. Of course his 'trade' is passé, thus his dam is worth nothing.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Worth a shot</span> - If something is worth a shot, it is worth trying as there is some chance of success.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Worth your salt</span> - Someone who is worth their salt deserves respect.
				</div>

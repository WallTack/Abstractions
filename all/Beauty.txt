									<h2 id="_idioms" class="header">Idioms</h2>
			<div id="idioms" class="subContainer">
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Age before beauty</span> - When this idiom is used, it is a way of allowing an older person to do something first, though often in a slightly sarcastic way.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Beauty is in the eye of the beholder</span> - Beauty is in the eye of the beholder means that different people will find different things beautiful and that the differences of opinion don't matter greatly.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Beauty is only skin deep</span> - This idiom means that appearances can be deceptive and something that seems or looks good may turn out to be bad.
				</div>

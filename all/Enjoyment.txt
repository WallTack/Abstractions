								<div class="dName">Chambers's Twentieth Century Dictionary</div>
				<div class="etymology">
					O. Fr. <i>enjoier</i>, to give joy to—<i>en</i> (=L. <i>in</i>), and <i>joie</i>, joy; or O. Fr. <i>enjoir</i>, to enjoy—<i>en</i>, and <i>joir</i>—L. <i>gaudēre</i>, to rejoice.

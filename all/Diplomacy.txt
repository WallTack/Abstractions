									<h2 id="_idioms" class="header">Idioms</h2>
			<div id="idioms" class="subContainer">
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Gunboat diplomacy</span> - If a nation conducts its diplomatic relations by threatening military action to get what it wants, it is using gunboat diplomacy.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>
								<div class="idioms">
					<span>Megaphone diplomacy</span> - If negotiations between countries or parties are held through press releases and announcements, this is megaphone diplomacy, aiming to force the other party into adopting a desired position.
				</div>	
				<span class="separatorSmall">***</span>

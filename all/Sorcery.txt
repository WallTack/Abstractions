												<h2 id="_etymology" class="header">Etymology</h2>
			<div id="etymology" class="subContainer">
								<div class="dName">Webster's Revised Unabridged Dictionary</div>
				<div class="etymology">
					OE. <span class="wUets">sorcerie</span>, OF. <span class="wUets">sorcerie</span>, fr. OF. &amp; F. <span class="wUets">sorcier</span>, a sorcerer, LL. <span class="wUets">sortiarius</span>, fr. L. <span class="wUets">sors</span>, <span class="wUets">sortis</span>, a lot, decision by lot, fate, destiny. See <span class="wUer">Sort</span> (n.) 
				</div>	
								<div class="dName">Chambers's Twentieth Century Dictionary</div>
				<div class="etymology">
					O. Fr. <i>sorcerie</i>—Low L. <i>sortiarius</i>, one who tells fortunes by lots—L. <i>sortīri</i>, to cast lots—<i>sors</i>, <i>sortis</i>, a lot.

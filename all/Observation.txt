In those sciences which describe and explain provinces of the universe as it exists, such as astronomy and systematic biology, observations are, for the most part, made under circumstances or conditions which may be selected, but cannot be produced at will. But in those sciences which analyze the behavior of substances under various conditions it is customary first to place the object to be examined under artificially produced conditions, and then to make an observation upon it. This whole performance, of which the observation is a part, is called an experiment. Formerly sciences were divided into sciences of experiment and sciences of observation, meaning observation without experiment. But now experiments are made in all sciences. It is only occasionally that the word observation has been used to imply the absence of experimentation.</span> </li><li><span class="tSpeech">n</span> <span class="term">observation</span> <span class="exp">The result of such a scientific practice; the information gained by observing: as, to tabulate observations.</span> </li><li><span class="tSpeech">n</span> <span class="term">observation</span> <span class="exp">Knowledge; experience.</span> </li><li><span class="tSpeech">n</span> <span class="term">observation</span> <span class="exp">A remark, especially a remark based or professing to be based on what has been observed; an opinion expressed.</span> </li><li><span class="tSpeech">n</span> <span class="term">observation</span> <span class="exp">The fact of being seen or noticed; notice; remark: as, to escape observation; anxious to avoid observation.</span> </li><li><span class="tSpeech">n</span> <span class="term">observation</span> <span class="exp">Observance; careful attention to rule, custom, or precept, and performance of whatever is prescribed or required.</span> </li><li><span class="tSpeech">n</span> <span class="term">observation</span> <span class="exp">A rite; a ceremony; an observance.</span> </li><li><span class="tSpeech">n</span> <span class="term">observation</span> <span class="exp">Synonyms Observance, Observation.
